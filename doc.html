1. Abstract

Web Gardening is a web application that has the function of a digital assistant for growing spring flowers.

2. Introduction

Introducing Web Gardening, the perfect web app for anyone looking to add some color to their garden this spring. Web Gardening offers a wide range of beautiful flowers for customers to choose from and tips for gardeners.

2.1. Intended Audience

Web Gardening is destined for two kinds of users: gardeners and customers.

For gardeners, Web Gardening makes it easy to add the perfect flowers from your garden. They can add your spring flowers like tulips, daffodils, hyacinths, and crocuses, and get tips on planting and caring for their blooms.

For customers, Web Gardening offers a convenient way to purchase high-quality flowers online. They can browse through the available options, read reviews from other customers, and place their order with just a few clicks.

2.2. Purpose

This application's purpose is to help gardeners harvest, grow and sell their spring flowers. Customers can view the variety of products and choose which to buy. It is a useful application for both users, especially for gardeners who can receive tips about their cultures.

3. Structure

The application is structured in multiple pages: Home page, About, Info, Products, Profile, Login, Register, and Check out.

3.1.Home Page (index.html)

This is the main page of the application. When the users open our app, this page welcomes them into our world. On the top of the page, they have access to the menu of the application. If they click on the "Get your favorite flowers now" button it redirects them to the products page.

3.2. About (about.html)

This page tells the users our purpose and their possibilities. It has a description of our web application. At the bottom of the page, they can see some reviews from our users.

3.3. Info (info.html)

This page helps the new users accommodate to our application. It has listed all the possibilities for each of our users (gardener/customer).

3.4. Products (products.html)

This page displays the products that our gardeners are selling. The customers can scroll through the page until they add to their cart every flower they like.

3.5. Login (login.html)

Here the users can log into their accounts. All they need is to type their email and their password.

3.6. Register (register.html)

Here the new users can make an account. All they need is to type an email, a password, their phone number and tell us if they are a gardener or a customer.

3.7. Check Out (checkOut.html)

Going through this page is the last step to placing their order. After they type their info, the flowers are on their way to them.

3.7. Order Placed (done.html)

After completing their info and clicking on the "Place your order" button, they will see this page which tells them that they successfully placed their order.

4. How To Use?

4.1. As A Gardener

For a gardener, Web Gardening makes it easy to add the perfect flowers from their garden. After they create an account and log in, they can add their spring flowers like tulips, daffodils, hyacinths, and crocuses, and get tips on caring for their blooms based on some sensors. Then, when the flowers are ready, they can sell them!

4.2. As A Customer

The customers can browse through the available options in the Products section, read reviews from other customers in the About section, and place their order with just a few clicks.
