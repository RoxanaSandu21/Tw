how to use our web app?

You are new to our app and don't know where to start? No problem!

If you are a customer, let me give you a tour. You can browse through the available options in the Products section, read reviews from other customers in the About section, and place your order with just a few clicks. Don't forget to create an account and login in order to buy your favorite flowers!

If you are a gardener, Web Gardening makes it easy to add the perfect flowers from your garden. After you create an account and login, you can add your spring flowers like tulips, daffodils, hyacinths, and crocuses, and get tips on caring for your blooms based on some sensors. Then, when they are ready, you can sell them!
